Project 1 Proposal: C19Relief

Problem Description

The number of coronavirus cases in the United States has continued to grow exponentially in the past few months, topping the chart globally. Abiding by the CDC guidelines, most people are wearing face masks, keeping their social distances, and restaurants along with other entertainment facilities have delayed opening. However, the nation’s cases are still soaring. As members of the community, we want to address this concern by bringing in some measures other countries have attempted to efficiently “flatten the curve.” For instance, in China, COVID-testing applications and softwares are available in most social media platforms. There are ways people can not only get “self-tested” by filling out a survey and purchasing basic preventive products such as face masks and gloves, but can also gain access to first-hand, reliable sources of information on surrounding cases and news by neighborhood. As students, we fully acknowledge our limited ability to outright prevent the spread of viruses. However, we want to build such a platform as a means of making information more transparent among American citizens. Existing web applications for tracking COVID cases have been limited. For example, the Johns Hopkins University COVID tracker focuses on COVID tracing. Its emphasis on states and territories make it more difficult for users to visualize in comparison to their local neighborhoods. Amazon offers face masks and other preventative products to purchase, yet their product offerings are limited, with no source to compile and contrast similar products from multiple websites. Furthermore, MAYO Clinic provides a short survey for users to self-test for COVID-19. Resources like these are scattered in a manner that is hard to locate, and thus, can serve as an inconvenience for people desperately looking for help. For these reasons, we want to build a platform that serves as the center of information, where people can gain access to different types of COVID-19 related, first-hand resources.

App Description

This website is targeted towards anyone, most likely within the United States, to gain access to information that is pertinent to them. For our users, there are many options available once they are on the site. The main functionality will include the ability to visualize their neighborhood’s latest COVID-19 updates. Specifically, how many people are infected and where they are located. The site will also provide other functions such as allowing users to purchase products used for preventative measures. We will allow them to search for a compilation of different products to purchase at a low rate. We will also be linking additional resources such as the latest news, potential rumors about COVID-19 related news, as well as self-testing surveys from different verified healthcare providers. Because all of the current available data for COVID confirmed cases by zip codes are compiled individually by state, we plan on compiling all of these data together so that users are able to more easily track and monitor their health within local neighborhoods.
